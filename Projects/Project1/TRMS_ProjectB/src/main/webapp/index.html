Welcome to Versalife!

Versalife is dedicated to creating solutions to the world's problems before they arise. Some of our research in the past has been dedicated to the augmentation technologies such as neuropozyne and mechanical augmentations of the past. We are currently looking into the "Gray-Death" epidemic that has been afflicting the world. To combat this new plague Versalife is fully concentrating on Ambrosia (the vaccine for the Gray Death). Although we have the solution it does not mean that we can manufacture it so easily. Any donations will be greatly appreciated. Versalife will strive for the benefit of mankind.


Meet Our Founder Bob Page

Versalife was founded by Bob Page in 1999. Our company has grown quit since our early days. Originally based in New York City, this year Mr. Page has brought our company to be now based in Hong Kong. What started as one man's dream to bring medical aid to United States has grown to become a world-wide organization dedicated to revolutionize the world.

We make tomorrow look like yesterday!
-Bob Page, Founder of Versalife
